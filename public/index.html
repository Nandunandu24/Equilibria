SYSTEM SYSTEMICS

Decision Science Flow

01

Data Ingestion & Synthesis Layer

Aggregates 120-day baseline transaction tables, initial stock levels, and active lead time indices.

  • Compiles pricing, sales volumes, promotions, and weather disruptions.
  • Injects noise parameters to reflect real-world logistics bottlenecks.
02

Predictive Analytics Core (OLS Model)

Fits sales logs using Ordinary Least Squares: Q = α + βP + γX + ε.

  • Calculates Price Elasticity of Demand coefficient dynamically.
  • Evaluates statistical p-values, standard errors, and model R-squared metrics.
03

Stochastic Simulation & Optimization Core

Evaluates pricing and inventory strategies over a 14-day window under supply chain risks.

  • Runs 300 Monte Carlo trials simulating lognormal queue lead times.
  • Searches decision grid (Pricing & safety stock) to maximize net profit.
04

Multi-Agent Debate & Consensus Layer

Orchestrates debate between persona agents to build executive-ready policy directives.

  • Supply Chain Risk Agent seeks to protect buffer levels.
  • Financial Controller Agent minimizes warehouse cost overheads.
  • Strategy Commander Agent resolves tradeoffs and commits policy settings.
